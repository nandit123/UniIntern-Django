About UniIntern

The aim for the UniIntern is to manage the internship process in the university effectively. This website is aimed at university students, companies and as well as the university authorities as they are the key entities involved in the internship process. This project is an ERP system for the university to manage the same. The key resources here are the internship, infrastructure, students, and the companies.
